This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones this website uses, and how you can control them — in accordance with Spanish Law 34/2002 (LSSI-CE) and the EU ePrivacy Directive.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences and behaviour across visits.

2. Cookies Used on This Website

This website uses only strictly necessary cookies to function correctly. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Cookie Purpose Type Duration
lang Remembers your selected language (EN / ES / CA) Functional Persistent (localStorage)
darkMode Remembers your light/dark mode preference Functional Persistent (localStorage)

Both values are stored in your browser's localStorage, not as traditional cookies. They are never transmitted to any server and contain no personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may include a link to cal.com for scheduling meetings. If you visit that service, it operates under its own cookie and privacy policies.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can clear localStorage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. Here's how to do it in the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data

Clearing this data will reset your language and theme preferences on this website.

5. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Cookie Policy if new functionality is added to the site. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
